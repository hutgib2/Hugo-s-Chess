chess reboot pieces/replacements: 
1. legionary replaces pawn
2. dragon replaces knight
3. archer replaces queen
4. wizard replaces bishop
5. catapult replaces rook
6. emperor replace king

legionary:
can only move one even on its first move and there's 8 of them.
can only move and kill forwards. 
can promote to archer if it reaches other sides of map.
cannot be killed from the front thanks to its shield.

dragon:
can move and kill up to 2 spaces in all directions per turn and there's 2 of them.
can move through enemy piece if there's an empty tile.
can kill by attacking everything in front of and diagonally forwards 1 space but cannot move on same turn

archer:
only 1 archer on board and can move 1 tile in all directions per turn but cannot kill and move on same turn.
can kill an enemy up to to 3 tiles away in 8 different directions away every turn but no one can move on the same turn.
has to move to move to reload. when it dies it heals the emperor back to full health.

wizard:
only 2 wizards on board can move 1 tile in all directions per turn but cannot move an opponent on same turn.
can move any enemy and ally piece to any empty tile and has to move to reload.
cannot move stunned enemies and allies and there are 2 of them.
when it dies it can mind control a opponent piece for one turn.

catapult:
can move 2 tiles in all directions per turn but cannot stun on same turn.
can stun any enemy to any tile for 2 turns but has to move to shoot again.
cannot stun enemies that were just moved and there are 2 of them.

emperor:
only one emperor and can move like the queen piece but 
player will lose if the emperor is put in checkmate.
the empereror has 3 lives, if a player lands on it, they will take one life away and go back to the spot they were before.

chess board is still the same.
