chess reboot pieces/replacements: 
1. legionary replaces pawn
2. dragon replaces knight
3. archer replaces queen
4. wizard replaces bishop
5. catapult replaces rook
6. emperor replace king

legionary:
can only move one even on its first move and there's 8 of them.
can only move and attack forwards. 
can promote to archer if it reaches other sides of map.
cannot be attacked from the front thanks to its shield.

dragon:
can move and attack up to 2 spaces in all directions per turn and there's 2 of them.
can move through enemy piece if there's an empty tile.
can attack by attacking everything in front of and diagonally forwards 1 space but cannot move on same turn

archer:
only 1 archer on board and can move 1 tile in all directions per turn but cannot attack and move on same turn.
can attack an enemy up to to 3 tiles away in 8 different directions away every turn but no one can move on the same turn.
has to move to move to reload. when it dies it heals the emperor back to full health.

wizard:
only 2 wizards on board can move and attack to 1 tile in all directions per turn.
can swap places with any ally and enemy piece unless it's either stunned, an emperor or another wizard.
when it dies, undecided.

catapult:
can move 2 tiles in all directions per turn but cannot stun on same turn.
can stun any enemy in front of it for 1 turn but has to move to shoot again.
cannot stun enemies that were just moved and there are 2 of them.

emperor:
only one emperor and can move like the queen piece but 
player will lose if the emperor is put in checkmate.
the empereror has 3 lives, if a player lands on it, they will take one life away and go back to the spot they were before.

chess board is still the same.
